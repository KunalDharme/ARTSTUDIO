## About

ArtStudio is a web platform built for sharing and discovering visual artwork.

Users can upload images and videos, showcase their creations, explore artwork from others, and participate in a simple marketplace experience for buying and selling art.

The platform is open to everyone — from professional artists to hobby creators and individuals exploring their creativity.

---

## ✨ Features

* Upload and showcase artwork
* Support for image and MP4/WebM media
* Browse artwork with tags and pagination
* View uploaded photos and videos directly in the browser
* Buy and sell artwork
* Discover creations from independent artists and creators

---

## 🎯 Who is ArtStudio for?

ArtStudio is designed for anyone interested in art and creativity.

Whether you create professionally, enjoy art as a hobby, or simply want to share your ideas visually, ArtStudio provides a place to publish and explore artwork.
